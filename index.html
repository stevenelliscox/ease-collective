The Aviation Collection

It started with an F-15.

A fellow squadron wife asked Ani to design a piece inspired by the jet her husband flew. It was a hit — word spread through the F-15 community, then to squadrons across the country. Today the Aviation Collection turns the aircraft you love into hand-sawn sterling silver and gold-filled keepsakes, each one made to order.
