Sizing guide

Bracelets, bangles & cuffs

Wrap a soft tape measure (or a strip of paper you then measure against a ruler) snugly around your wrist, just below the wrist bone. That measurement is your wrist size — for bangles, add about 1" so it slides over your hand; for cuffs and adjustable bracelets, order your measured wrist size.

Rings

Wrap a strip of paper around the base of your finger, mark where it overlaps, and measure the length in millimeters. As a quick guide: 51.5 mm = US 6, 54 mm = US 7, 56.5 mm = US 8. Measure at the end of the day when fingers are warm, and size up if you’re between sizes.

Necklace lengths

16" sits at the base of the neck, 18" rests just below the collarbone, and 20" falls onto the upper chest — perfect for layering.

Still unsure? Send us a note and we’ll help you find the right fit.
